Trending Jewellery Types

  • Bead Jewellery: These are the accessories with beads, which have different designs, sizes and materials, like gold, silver, brass, copper, plastic, etc.
  • Temple Jewellery: It got its name from Bharatnatyam dancers and is inspired by Hindu gods and goddesses. It has fantastic motif designs and is found in many different materials.

  • Artificial Jewellery: Artificial or Imitation Jewellery is made of artificial material and is very cost-effective. If you don’t want to invest in gold jewellery, go for it.
  • Handmade Jewellery: This type of accessory is made with hands, and no machines are involved in its making process. Designing it requires a lot of skill and time and is flawless.
  • Kundan Jewellery: Kundan jewellery is a gemstone that looks very beautiful in accessories. It has a unique shine and will win your heart in one go.


Tips to Take Care of Your Jewellery

  • To prevent your jewellery from scratches, dullness, and tarnishing, it is essential to take care of them properly.
  • We would suggest you keep it away from Wood because it may stain your accessories or speed up its tarnishing process.
  • Store it in a Ziploc bag to keep it away from air and moisture.
  • Additionally, it would be best to keep your jewellery from skincare, like lotions, perfumes, and hairsprays.
  • Avoid leaving them in sunlight to prevent dullness.
  • Give your jewellery a good wipe using an anti-tarnish cloth after every usage.
  • To clean your jewellery at home, use dishwashing soap and warm water and then rub them with a soft cloth. Dry it properly in the end.
  • Last but not least, keep your artificial jewellery away from genuine pieces. For real ones, use soft cloth bags for storing.
